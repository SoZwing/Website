A few of my artworks. I’m interested in what makes us who we are, from cultural and familial heritage to the stories (and history) that built our mind and our lives. With word plays, with common objects alterations, I interrogate our behavior patterns, our collective and individual history, and how it impacts our ability to act or to feel free. And, as everyone, I love to take pictures of sunsets and dolphins (Everyone does that, right?)
